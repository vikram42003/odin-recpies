The Strawberry Banana High-Protein Shake

Here comes something to quench your thirst, taste and protein requirements.

"This high-protein shake packed with omega-3 fatty acids can be enjoyed for breakfast or as an anytime meal replacement."
~ The Liz Army

I present to you a link leading to the original recipe, courtesy of The Liz Army.

Prep Time: 15mins Total Time: 15mins Servings: 1

Nutritional Info (per serving)

Calories: 366 Fat: 12g Carbs: 43g Protein: 27g

Ingredients

  • 1½ teaspoons flax seeds
  • 1½ cup soy milk or any milk
  • 1½ cup orange juice
  • 1½ banana
  • 4 frozen strawberries
  • 1 scoop whey protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon flaxseed oil

Steps to make Strawberry Banana Shake

Step 1

Place flax seeds in a food processor or coffee grinder; pulse until ground.

Step 2

Combine soy milk, orange juice, banana, strawberries, whey protein powder, ground flax seeds, and flax seed oil in a blender; blend until smooth.

And thats it, Enjoy your refreshing shake!
